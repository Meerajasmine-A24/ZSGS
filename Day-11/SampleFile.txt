Zoho Corporation is a global technology company headquartered in India. It is best known for its suite of cloud-based business tools such as Zoho CRM, Zoho Mail, Zoho Books, and Zoho Projects. The company focuses on privacy, user data protection, and building software in-house without relying on third-party advertising. Founded in 1996 by Sridhar Vembu, Zoho has grown organically and is known for its unique philosophy of bootstrapped growth, product innovation, and employee-centric culture.
